About Me

I am an individual deeply passionate about the intersection of technology, nature, and personal growth. Proficient in Linux and open source software, I thrive on collaborative innovation and customization. Exploring the great outdoors through hiking, motorized surfboarding, and yoga invigorates me. I actively pursue self-improvement through meta learning and find joy in experimenting with new experiences. With an open mind and a zest for life, my aim is to inspire others to embrace their passions and discover the interconnectedness of diverse interests.
